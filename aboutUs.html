How R&R's Eggciting Eggs began

R&R's Eggciting Eggs began in 2016, on a small lifestyle block when a young boy was surprised by his parents with some chicks from the animal shelter. Every day he fed them, gave them water and took good care of them. A year later, the chickens started laying eggs, and the boy had an idea - "Why don't I sell the eggs?" And with that one question, R&R's Eggciting eggs began.
